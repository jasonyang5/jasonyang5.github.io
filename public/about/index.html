About Me#

Hi Everyone! I’m Jason Yang, a 4th year Economics Major and Math Minor at the University of Chicago.

I’ve produced some (probably mediocre) research in Financial Economics as an Undergrad and currently love reading interesting articles published on the American Economic Review, Journal of Political Economy, and Journal of Finance.

Coffee is my big hobby; I currently roast and brew single origin coffees from around the world.

About the Blog#

I have the blog divided by Category and Tag

  • Coffee
    • CoffeeLong: Longer posts about coffee. Reviews of green coffee & roasted coffee, Reviews of equipment, unique recipes, etc
    • CoffeeUpdate: Short updates on what beans or equipment just came in the mail and what I think about them
  • Economics
    • MyResearch: Summaries and links to my papers (co-)written as an undergrad
    • Papers: Summaries of interesting peer-reviewed papers published by professional economists

None of the links on this site are affiliate links. I am not an influencer (yet?), I just like writing about coffee and economics.
