Local Ethiopia Yirgacheffe

March 18, 2021 · Me

I was surprised to find a bag of beans from Ethiopia Yirgacheffe roasted yesterday at a local roastery (Kusanya Cafe) at Whole Foods. The beans had a very smooth mouthfeel and were very bright and fruity.

I tried V60 first with the tabbed & bleached paper filters. The coffee felt quite thin but definitely not lacking in personality.

The Clever Dripper with a cloth filter tasted similar but had a much fuller body (which I personally prefer) because of the additional dissolved oils.

Kusanya Cafe is a non profit cafe and roastery just 5 streets south from my apartment. I will definitely be ordering from them again (this time directly from the cafe).

Settings:

  • V60, Tabbed and Bleached Paper Filters from Hario
    • Grind #27 on Breville Smart Grinder Pro
    • 15g coffee 250 ml water, fresh off boil
    • 45g + 45s bloom
    • 105s total bloom time
    • 180s total brew time
  • Clever, Coffee Sock #4 Melitta Filter
    • Grind #28 on Breville SGP
    • 15g coffee 250ml water
    • 2m infusion time, ~50s drawdown time
