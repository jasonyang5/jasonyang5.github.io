Analyzing Politics Review

April 28, 2021 · Me

General Impressions#

Recently I’ve been reading a book called “Analyzing Politics” by Kenneth A Shepsle, which introduces rational models in political science. Rational models seek to explain political behavior (e.g. group voting, legislation, etc.) using individuals with “rational preferences”. Because I’ve waded through an economics major, I felt at home with this book’s treatment of the subject. My next few blog posts will cover a few topics I found interesting.
