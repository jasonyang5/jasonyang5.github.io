Best United Miles Redemption Ever? (+ bonus rant)

June 19, 2021 · Me

Credit card blogs love to recommend buying first class flights abroad to get the most ‘value’ out of your points, citing 2 to 5 cents per point in some cases.

I think I’ve got them beat. Try 9 cents per point for a United Economy flight to Shanghai next April…

Cash Flights: NYC to Shanghai in April 2022

United Miles Flights NYC to Shanghai in April 2022

The Bonus Rant: It’s easy to get caught up in credit card marketing with their crazy miles valuations, sometimes citing the best case with crazy expensive international flight redemptions. These valuations are too simplistic and make some cards appear a lot better than they actually are. But, I personally would never pay full price in cash for a first class international flight with cash (and I don’t think I’m alone in this). After adjusting for my willingness to pay, the valuation of the first class redemption drops back down to 1.2 to 1.5 cents per point.
